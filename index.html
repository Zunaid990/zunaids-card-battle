Zunaids Card Battle ⚔️

Welcome to Zunaids Card Battle! Click "New Deck" to get a fresh deck of cards, then click "Deal Cards" to deal two cards—one for each player. The player with the higher card value wins the round. Card values rank as follows: Ace (14), King (13), Queen (12), Jack (11), and 2-10 as their face value. Suits don’t affect the outcome. Click "Reset Game" to start over. Let the battle begin!
